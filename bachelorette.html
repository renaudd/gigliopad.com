La Bellissima Bachelorette

Elegance, sunshine, and excessive amounts of pasta. Welcome to the ultimate Roman Bachelorette at Gigliopad.

Day One
Cooking class followed by night clubs.
Day Two
Aerials class or yoga, Galeria Borghese, nail salon, stand-up comedy or live music.
Day Three
Bike the Appian Way or wine-tasting tour. Or, bike the appian way to the vineyards.
Day Four
Spa day at Therma Oasi or Terme dei Papi. For something closer to Rome, QC Termeroma.
Day Five
Vatican or Colosseum or Spanish Steps with shopping. Evening picnic at the rose garden.
