Quintessential Rome meets modern artsy energy. Giglio Pad is a sophisticated hub nestled in one of the city's most vibrant rioni. We're just one block from Campo de' Fiori—the perfect spot for morning coffee or late-night exploration.

Designed with a creative touch, this top-floor residence features unique boutique-style "sleeping nooks," a high-capacity electric supply (6kW), and central A/C to keep you cool during the Roman summer.

  • THE VIBE Artsy, authentic, inherently cool.
  • LOCATION One block from Campo de' Fiori.
  • COMFORT 6kW Power / central A/C & Heating.
  • LAYOUT Boutique cabin-style sleeping alcoves.
  • AMENITIES Dishwasher, Washer/Dryer in unit.
  • HERITAGE The former home of painter Ramón Gaya.
