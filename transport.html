Getting Around

Arrivals (No Stress)

Only take official White Taxis from FCO. The fare is fixed at €55—confirm this with the driver by asking "Tariffa fissa?" before you leave. Skip the Leonardo Express if you’re tired; instead, take the FL1 regional train to Stazione Trastevere (€8) and then hop on Tram 8 to Arenula/Cairoli. It brings you right to the neighborhood edge without the Termini madness.

The 40 Express

Need to hit Termini? Take Bus 40 Express from Corso Vittorio. It’s fast, cheap (€1.50), and way better than the Metro. Just tap your card on the bus.
