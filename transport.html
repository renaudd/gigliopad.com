Getting Around

Arrivals

Grab a fixed-rate taxi from FCO (€55) or take the Leonardo Express to Termini and then a taxi. Uber Black is your best bet for central city moves. Taxis are always waiting at Largo di Torre Argentina.

The 40 Express

Need to hit Termini? Take Bus 40 Express from Corso Vittorio. It’s fast, cheap (€1.50), and way better than the Metro. Just tap your card on the bus.
