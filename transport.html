Getting Around

The Regola Hub

Walking is the only way to truly see Rome. From Gigliopad, you can hit the Pantheon in 10 minutes, the Jewish Ghetto in 5, and the best gelato in the world in about 3. Forget the bus.

Crossing Bridges

Walk across Ponte Sisto and you’re in the heart of Trastevere. It’s the perfect loop for a morning coffee or a sunset beer by the river. It’s where Rome actually happens.

Arrivals

Grab a fixed-rate taxi from FCO (€50) or take the Leonardo Express to Termini and then a 15-minute taxi to Vicolo del Giglio. Pro tip: Don't bring a huge suitcase; the streets are historic (meaning: bumpy).
